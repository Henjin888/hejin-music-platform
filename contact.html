Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start investing in artists?

Simply browse our discover page, find an artist you believe in, and click "Invest Now". You can start with as little as $10.

How do artists receive funding?

Funds are held in escrow via smart contracts and released to artists as they hit predetermined milestones.

What returns can I expect as an investor?

Returns vary based on artist success. Investors receive a share of streaming revenue, merchandise sales, and other income proportional to their investment.
